Purpose

Microservices Mastery is a learning companion for developers who want to master microservices architecture through a Reinforcement Learning-inspired approach.

Instead of passively reading tutorials, you learn by doing, failing, reflecting, and iterating — just like an RL agent training in an environment.

Each episode gives you hands-on challenges, rewards good architectural patterns, and penalizes anti-patterns. Your "policy" (decision-making intuition) improves over time through real experience.

How It Works
🎮
Episodes

Structured learning steps with increasing difficulty (curriculum learning)

🏆
Rewards

Points for best practices (circuit breakers, observability, resilience)

⚠️
Penalties

Negative rewards for anti-patterns (shared DB, tight coupling, no timeouts)

🔄
Reflection

Experience Replay — revisit old episodes with new knowledge

📊
Progress Tracking

Visualize your learning journey and watch your policy converge
